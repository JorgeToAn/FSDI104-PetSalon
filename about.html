About us

From a family business

Pixel Pets Salon started as a family business in 2016, we would take care of the pets from the neighbors at our own home and soon enough more and more visits were being scheduled with us, and so the business grew until we opened our first local.

Six years later, this has become a passion of ours and we would love to keep this going for years and years to come.

Values we hold

  • Respect: We care for your pets as much as you do, we wouldn't do anything you woudln't do yourself.
  • Love: Treating your pets with love makes them feel at ease and makes them happy.
  • Money: Cause we love moneyyyy money money money money.
